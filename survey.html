Survey Introduction

My name is Nataraj Munoli and I am a graduate student at Rochester Institute of Technology. As a part of my graduate thesis, I have developed a model for New Product Development process; 3D Hybrid Model. My review of current New Product Development models indicates that most companies follow a unique Product Development Process which is tailored specifically to what works for the company, and these models are variations of either Cooper’s Stage Gate Method, Suh’s Axiomatic design theory, Boehm’s Spiral model or a combination of all. These models are currently used by most industries despite their limitations and bottlenecks. They do not address all the stages of Product Development Process, and this does not allow the company to tailor the process to fit their needs in all aspects of the development process. I have combined the principles of these three models and developed the 3D Hybrid Model with the aim to help industries overcome some or most of the limitations and bottlenecks faced during a NPD process.
